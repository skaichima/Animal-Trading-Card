Snowy Owl

PET / MESSANGER

Snowy Owl is a very large messanger with a smoothly rounded head. It can fly fast and in long distances.With snowy owl you won't miss any messages.

Scientific name

Mass

origin
Snowy owls are native to the Arctic regions of both North America and the Palearctic, breeding mostly on the tundra. It has a number of unique adaptations to its habitat and lifestyle, which are quite distinct from other extant owls.
Enemies
As far as natural predators go, only a handful of animals hunt Snowy Owls, usually foxes and wolves.
